About JYZ COMPUTERS

JYZ COMPUTERS is a pioneering digital services company based in Kenya, dedicated to providing innovative and affordable digital solutions to small and medium enterprises (SMEs) and individuals.

The Problem

Despite the rapid growth of digital technology in Kenya, many SMEs struggle to harness its potential due to:

  • High Costs: Premium rates that are unaffordable for small businesses
  • Technical Expertise: Lack of in-house expertise for online presence
  • Access to Resources: Limited access to reliable internet and digital tools

Our Solution

JYZ COMPUTERS addresses these issues through:

  • Affordable Pricing: Competitive pricing without compromising quality
  • Comprehensive Packages: Bundled services covering all digital needs
  • Local Support: Robust support system with locally-based experts
